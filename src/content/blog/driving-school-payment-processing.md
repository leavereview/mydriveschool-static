## Payment Options to Offer

### Credit/Debit Cards

Essential. Most students expect to pay by card online.

### Package Prepayment

Sell lesson packages. Get paid upfront.

### Payment Plans

Optional. Can help with expensive packages but adds complexity.

## Best Practices

### Collect Payment Upfront

Prepayment reduces no-shows and improves cash flow.

### Automate Invoicing

Let software generate and send invoices automatically.

### Track Balances

Know who owes what at a glance.

### Send Payment Reminders

Automated reminders for outstanding balances.

## Reducing Processing Fees

- Encourage package purchases (fewer transactions)
- Consider bank transfer for large amounts
- Pass on fees where appropriate
