Effective student management is key to running a successful driving school.

## What Is Student Management?

Tracking everything about your students: contact info, progress, payments, documents, and communications.

## Key Components

### Student Profiles

- Contact information
- Emergency contacts
- Permit details
- Special notes

### Progress Tracking

- Hours completed
- Skills mastered
- Areas needing work
- Instructor notes

### Document Storage

- Permits
- Signed waivers
- Certificates

### Lesson History

Complete record of all lessons, cancellations, and outcomes.

## Benefits of Good Student Management

- Better student outcomes
- Easier compliance
- Professional image
- Faster onboarding of new instructors

## Parent Communication

Parents want to know how their kids are progressing. Good software provides:

- Parent login access
- Progress reports
- Lesson summaries
